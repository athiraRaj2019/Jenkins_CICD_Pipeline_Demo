* Create a new repository:
	- Click on Settings/Gear icon. 
	- Go to Repositories.
	- Click on Create Repository
	- Choose “maven2 (hosted)” from the list
	- Enter the desired name (POC-repo)
	- Choose Version Policy as “Mixed”, Layout policy as “Permissive” and Deployment policy as “Allow Redeploy”. 
	  (These properties allow us to create a custom hierarchical directory structure to store artifacts in our repository.)
	- Click on Create repository. (The repository URL: http://35.200.146.29:8081/repository/POC-repo/)
